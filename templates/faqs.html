Frequently Asked Questions

What is the story behind this business?

Luxury Rent started with one goal: make premium fashion and accessories accessible for important moments without forcing long-term ownership.

How does the rental process work?

You browse products, choose dates, confirm booking, and receive your selected piece. After use, simply return it on the agreed return date.

What quality can I expect?

Every item is authenticity-checked, cleaned, and inspected before dispatch to ensure premium presentation and reliable condition.

What is your return policy?

Items should be returned by the due date in the same condition. Late returns or damage may result in extra charges as per policy.

Can I cancel a booking?

Yes, cancellation is allowed before dispatch. Refund eligibility depends on how close the cancellation is to the rental start date.
